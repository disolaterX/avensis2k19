    Sponsors

    Robotics

    LINE FOLLOWING ROBOT

    The objective is to make your own line following robot which can complete custom tracks accurately in the shortest amount of time. The autonomous robot will begin it's position from an initial marked position in a dark isolated room. It will follow the line strip beneath it and would cover the track. The robot has to lead it's path by sensing the line. The objective is to cover the entire track in least possible time with sufficient accuracy.

    ROBO WARS

    To build a autonomous robot which is capable of fighting another robot. Robot can have special abilities to fight another robot. The autonomous robot will be provided an arena to fight upon. There will be some time limit. The robot has to depart the another robot from the arena for a clear win.

    ROBO RUGBY

    Robo Rugby is an event in which robots try to score a point by hitting the balls into the scoring zone at both end points of a playing table. Involves two small robots and some balls of different colours. Every coloured ball clutches different points. One robot is assigned to each team and the position of each ball at the end of the game governs the final score and hence the winner.

    CARNAGE

    This is an event with an aim to construct an autonomous bot which can successfully pass through each of the hurdle in a specified order. The teams will compete on completing the course in the fastest time possible and rack up the most points which are awarded on each successful hurdle pass.

    ROBO MAZE

    The robots have to start from one end of the maze, make the required turns and move till the far end of the maze in minimum time and with minimum penalties possible, while covering all the mandatory paths.

    ROBOWORLD - AN EXHIBITION

    This event will have an exhibition of autonomous robots with different abilities. Bots will be having unique abilities, they will be judged on creativity and functionality of the bot. Maximum registrations for the exhibition is restricted to 10. The event will be judged by the teachers.

    Lit & Quiz

    Face The Masks

    Round 1: Unfolding The Curtains Participants get to see photographs of 5 different movie characters with a 30 seconds time slot provided for the entire event wherein they have accomplish the following tasks: 1. 2. 3. 4. Name of Actor Name of Character Name of the Movie Whether the character is protagonist or antagonist. Round 2: Momo-Fizz Pong It’s a game in which players can come in a team of 2-3 wherein they will encounter six cups randomly filled with consumable eatables (like Momo chutney, Chasni ,Extra Lemon Lime etc). A single player from a team has to shoot a ping pong ball in one of these cups with maximum of 3 attempts to a team. Under each of six cups there is a slip containing 3 questions (Quotation Competition, Riddles or Famous Dialogue Completion) out of which any 2 correct answers will do the job and the teams will fetch exciting rewards.( A challenging and exciting team task could also be given in the slip and upon competition prizes would be provided).

    Judge A Book By Its Cover

    Preliminary Round (Registration Day): Pictionary - The game will work as an icebreaker wherein the participants will have to draw a suitable picture according to a randomly chosen word. It will be a fun event which will serve a two fold purpose to enthral as well as screen the contestants for next round. Round 1: In this round a movie trailer (or a part of movie) will be shown to the participants and they will have to guess the plot of the movie (Bonus points will be awarded if they guess the cast , director etc. ). Whoever is closest to the plot proceeds to the next round. Round 2: In second round the participants will be given a book cover (Back and Front) and they have to guess the storyline or the end of the plot (whichever is suitable for that particular novel’s context). Bonus questions can be asked like whether the book is a best seller/ author’s name / year of publication etc.

    Meme Competition

    Participants will be given a random picture from the internet and will be asked to make a meme out of it. Picture can be any event , show or just a public place or news debate with the horizon wide open. One must show their creativity skills with a knack of pun and humour embedded without deviating from the situation given. The event can be judged by the audience’s response or via social media platforms.

    Management Quiz

    Management Quiz is here to take you through an array of management scenarios and corporate world trivia. Prelims : One mark for each question. Round 1 : Ten points for every correct answer. Fifteen points for every passed question. Round 2: Passing the question is not allowed rather pounce is allowed. Pounce opens other teams to attempt the question raised by writting down the answer and showing it to the quizmaster. Ten points for a correct question (also, if the question is answered by pounce), and Minus five points for incorrect answer in case of pounce. Round 3: Five points for every correct answer. Total time limit for each team is 1.5 minutes i.e. 90 seconds.

    Parakh - The case study competition

    Always believed in your business sense and analytical abilities? Always dreamt of being a management mastermind? Here's a chance to prove yourself! On the spot registration will take place from 10a. m. onwards on the day of the event. The event shall have two rounds.

    EXTEMPORE (Just A Minute)

    The participants will be given their topics through chits. They will have to speak on the given topic for at least 1 minute and 2 minutes at maximum. The preparation time given to each participant will be 1 minute. The topics once given to the participants will not be changed under any circumstances.

    CREATIVE WRITING

    There are no restrictions to the form of writing. (This includes poetry, story, articles etc.) The topics will be given on the spot. Time limit for the writing will be 1 hour. Only one entry per person is allowed Entries can be either in English or in Hindi.

    Technical

    PUBG:

    This is a team event in which each squad will battle it out in the battle royale. The winning squad will be decided by the last man standing method. The contestant who will survive till last will become winner.

    PIANO TILES:

    This is a solo event in which each participant will battle it out in a musical game mode. The participants will be judged on the basis of highscore. The rule is to tap the black tiles only, according to the rhythm that is played. The person with high and accurate tapping speed will surely have an advantage over the others.

    2 CARS:

    This is a solo event in which each participant will have to compete with each other in this game. The rule is to control two cars at a same time on your screen(red and blue) in which you have to take circles and avoid squares. The participant with the highest score wins.
